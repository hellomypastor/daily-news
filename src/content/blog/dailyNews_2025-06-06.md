发布时间：2025-06-06 23:04:51

### [AI辅导助推德州私立学校跻身全国前2%：教育模式的颠覆性尝试](https://www.foxnews.com/media/texas-private-schools-use-ai-tutor-rockets-student-test-scores-top-2-country)
